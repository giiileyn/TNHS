Thank You for Your Support!

We would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude to all the volunteers, staff, and supporters who made this year's Brigada Heroes a great success. Your contributions are invaluable and have made a significant impact on our school community. Together, we are making TNHS School a better place for everyone!

The Brigada at Tabason National High School brings a range of impactful events, starting with the Brigada Liga 22' basketball games, where students come together to compete and showcase their skills. Free haircuts are offered to students as part of our community outreach efforts. We also feature Murang Ukay Ukay, where affordable second-hand clothing is made available for everyone.

This year, we hosted a Symposium on Vaccines and Monkeypox Awareness, focusing on health education and preventive measures. Our Hand Hygiene and Tooth Brushing campaigns were also launched to promote better personal health habits. Brigada Zumba 2022 encouraged physical fitness through fun dance routines for the students and staff.

Another exciting event was EMDAS GO, an interactive math program aimed at enhancing students' problem-solving skills. The Clean-Up Drive helped maintain a clean and safe environment for the entire school, while Brigada Pagbasa provided reading activities to encourage literacy.

Finally, we proudly launched the Mr. and Ms. Brigada 2022 Signature Campaign, a celebration of student talent and leadership in our school community. These events not only aim to foster camaraderie but also promote personal growth and awareness among our students.
