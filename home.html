Why Choose TNHS?

TNHS School provides quality education from Junior High School (Grades 7 to 10) to Senior High School with specialized strands. We are committed to nurturing students in both academic and practical fields, preparing them for a bright future.

Junior High School (Grades 7-10)

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Grade 7 to Grade 10, focusing on core academic subjects that lay the foundation for future learning.

Senior High School - GAS Strand

The General Academic Strand (GAS) is ideal for students who are undecided about their college path, providing a balanced approach to different fields of study.

Senior High School - TVL Strand

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Strand equips students with practical skills in various fields, ensuring they are job-ready after graduation.
