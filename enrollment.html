Enrollment at TNHS

Tabason National High School offers education for Grades 7 to 10 and Senior High School, with curricula focusing on General Academic Strand (GAS) and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL).

Junior High School (Grades 7 to 10)

Our Junior High School provides a strong foundation in core academic subjects and opportunities for personal growth and development. Students are nurtured to achieve academic excellence while building essential life skills.

Senior High School (GAS and TVL)

General Academic Strand (GAS): This strand offers a flexible curriculum ideal for students exploring different fields of interest, providing a balance of subjects across various disciplines.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL): Designed for students aiming to gain technical skills and certifications for employment or entrepreneurship.
