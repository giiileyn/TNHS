About TNHS School

Welcome to Tabason National High School, where excellence in education and character-building thrives. Our mission is to provide a holistic learning environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential.

Our History

Founded in 1985, TNHS School has a rich tradition of fostering academic excellence and nurturing talent. Over the years, we have become a beacon of quality education in the region, producing graduates who excel in various fields.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality education that shapes individuals into responsible, innovative, and globally competitive citizens.

Vision: To be recognized as a leading institution in secondary education, fostering a culture of academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors.
  • Respect: Fostering a culture of mutual respect and inclusivity.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking.

Our Facilities

TNHS School boasts modern facilities designed to enhance the learning experience, including:

  • State-of-the-art science and computer laboratories.
  • A well-stocked library with digital resources.
  • Spacious classrooms with multimedia capabilities.
  • Sports facilities including basketball courts, a gymnasium, and a soccer field.

Join Us in Shaping the Future

At TNHS School, we believe that education is the cornerstone of a brighter future. We invite you to be a part of our journey as we continue to inspire, educate, and empower the leaders of tomorrow.
